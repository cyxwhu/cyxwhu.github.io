Bio

I received my Ph.D. from The University of Chicago in 2020. Before that, I got bachelor degree from Wuhan University. I am interested in improving software availability and reliability, especially developing automated tools to help fix concurrency bugs. After graduation, I joined Meta as research scientist, helping expose Meta's data publicly safely and securely.
